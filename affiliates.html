Affiliate Program

Earn commissions by referring new curators and hub owners to 121212.community.

Last updated: March 14, 2025

About the Affiliate Program

The 121212.community Affiliate Program allows individuals and organizations to earn referral commissions by directing new users to the Platform who go on to win and pay for a city hub license. We believe in compensating those who help grow the ecosystem.

How It Works

  • Apply to the affiliate program by emailing love@121212.community with subject line "Affiliate Program Application"
  • Upon approval, you will receive a unique referral link and/or code
  • When a user clicks your link and successfully wins and pays for a hub license, you earn a commission
  • Commissions are tracked for 30 days from the initial click

Commission Structure

Approved affiliates earn a commission on the first-year license fee of any new curator referred through their unique link. Commission rates are disclosed upon program approval and are subject to change with reasonable notice to active affiliates.

Payment of Commissions

Commissions are paid monthly via ACH transfer or other approved payment method, provided the referred license has cleared any applicable refund window (typically 30 days from payment). Minimum payout threshold applies.

Prohibited Practices

Affiliates may not engage in:

  • Spam, unsolicited email, or deceptive marketing practices
  • Self-referral or referring your own accounts
  • Misrepresentation of the Platform, its services, or pricing
  • Paid search advertising using 121212 brand terms without written approval
  • Any practice that violates applicable laws or regulations

Disclosure Requirement

In accordance with FTC guidelines, affiliates must clearly disclose their relationship with 121212.community whenever promoting the Platform. A statement such as "I earn a commission if you sign up through my link" is required in all promotional contexts.

Termination

We reserve the right to terminate any affiliate relationship at our discretion, particularly for violations of these terms. Earned commissions on valid referrals will be paid out through the next payment cycle upon termination.

Apply

Interested in joining the affiliate program? Email love@121212.community with a brief description of your audience and how you plan to promote the Platform.
