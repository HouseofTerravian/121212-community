Accessibility

Our commitment to making 121212.community accessible to everyone.

Last updated: March 14, 2025

Our Commitment

MCM Enterprises is committed to ensuring that 121212.community is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards and continually work to improve the accessibility of our Platform.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure for compatibility with screen readers
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios across all text and UI elements
  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the Platform
  • Descriptive alt text for meaningful images
  • ARIA labels and roles where appropriate
  • Responsive design that works across device sizes and zoom levels
  • No content that flashes more than 3 times per second

Known Limitations

We are a growing platform and acknowledge that some areas may not yet fully meet accessibility standards. We actively work to identify and resolve accessibility issues as they are discovered or reported. Curator-published content on individual city hubs may vary in accessibility compliance, as that content is managed independently by hub operators.

Assistive Technology

Our Platform is designed to be compatible with common assistive technologies including screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver), magnification tools, and keyboard-only navigation. If you encounter compatibility issues, please let us know.

Feedback and Contact

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of 121212.community. If you experience any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: support@121212.community
  • Subject line: Accessibility Feedback

We aim to respond to accessibility inquiries within 3 business days.

Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to an accessibility concern, you may contact the relevant accessibility enforcement authority in your jurisdiction.
