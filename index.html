What is .community?

The Local Layer
of the 121212 Ecosystem

121212.community is where the ecosystem meets the ground. Each city subdomain is a living hub — curated by someone who knows it, loves it, and is invested in its Black community thriving.

🏙

You Own the Hub

Bid on your city's subdomain and become its curator. What you build there is yours — designed your way, monetized your way.

🔗

Gateway to the Full Ecosystem

Your hub connects visitors to everything 121212 — atlanta.121212.market, atlanta.121212.music, atlanta.121212.fun, and more. You're the front door.

📣

Curate What Matters

Interviews. Reviews. Event listings. Business directories. Top-10 food guides. Curate what drives culture, commerce, and community in your city.

The Process

Own Your City in 3 Steps

From bid to launch — here's how you claim your slice of the ecosystem.

01
🏷

Bid on Your City

Browse the marketplace and place your bid on any available city subdomain. Auctions run for up to 7 days. Highest bid wins the hub rights for one year.

02
🎨

Build Your Hub

Curate it your way — events, eats, interviews, business spotlights, community guides. Connect to other 121212 domains to give your city the full ecosystem experience.

03
🚀

Own the Culture

Monetize through listings, sponsorships, affiliate revenue, and community partnerships. Renew annually or let it ride — your hub, your terms.
